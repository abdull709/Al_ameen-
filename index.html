Our Story

Many Nigerians, including Non-Muslims, need to know more about Islam as a religion and a way of life. People are daily being fed with wrong notions about Islam. The need for us to address this problem is now, as the wrong notion is turning into an accepted norm. We can only do this by complimenting the efforts of our various religious groups and through our behavior’s as Muslims. This effort, in the form of Da’awah and exhibitions, will enable more converts to find it easy to embrace and practice Islam.

To buttress the above assertion, I wish to share my personal experience as a case study. I was born in the late 1950s in Mkar Gboko in Benue State, to a family which is predominantly Christian. I married a Police Officer Abdullahi Bugaje from Katsina, at the age of 14 immediately after primary school. I left my family and joined him in his numerous places of duties in different parts of the country. Together we raised 5 children. Unfortunately, my husband passes away in 1986 at the age of 46. The heavy burden of bringing up the children fell on my shoulders. Alhamdulillah, with determination and perseverance, the children were successfully raised in strict Islamic traditions. The result now is a successful family whose members include a General in the Nigerian Army, a Barrister at law, an Accountant, a Banker turn Director with Katsina State Education board. A Computer Analyst. Alhamdulillah, they are very successful in their individual fields of endeavors and are ever ready to contribute in the propagation of Islam. This experience has motivated me and the children to dedicate our lives in the cause of Allah through embarking on Da’awah programs. Am happy that in the Middle Belt part of Nigeria where I came from, many people are expressing zeal and commitments daily for knowledge to practice Islam. Many of my relations and friends have embrace Islam through me.

It is in realization of this that Al’Ameen Islamic Propagation Center was formed 2025. The pioneer members of this movement met formaly and sincerely for the purpose of promoting Islam with the full resolve to carry out this important responsibility in accordance with the laws of the land. The goal of the centre is to initiate, consolidate, encourage and promote Islamic education and worship. It is a great honor and privilege to write and introduce you to the above named Islamic propagation organization, its activities and ideals, conscious of your leading roles and commitment to Islamic causes and to invite you to participate as a patron.

The undersigned is the proprietor and initiator of the center and together with some individuals; they came up with this noble idea of inviting non-Muslim Nigerians to Islam. Conscious of my background and aware of the need to liberate my Christian relations from darkness to the light of Islam, I was propelled by the desire to establish and sustain this Islamic propagation body. The centre has now acquired parcels of lands in Gboko and Konshisha towns to build a Mosque, an Islamic Nursery and primary school and a Da’awah Center.

As a philanthropist and icon in propagating Islamic causes, it is most appropriate to keep you abreast of these developments. We also wish to solicit your kind approval to participate, support and contribute morally and materially to the substance and development of this worthy mission.

We shall be most grateful if you accept this request and welcome your contribution as this will be in line with Allah (SWT)’s injunction in the Holy Qur’an as quoted in Surat Anfaal 2-4 below Those only are believers whose hearts become full of fear when Allah is mentioned, and when His communications are recited to them they increase them in faith, and in their Lord do they trust. Those who keep up prayer and spend (benevolently) out of what we have given them. These are the believers in truth; they shall have from their Lord exalted grades and forgiveness and an honourable sustenance.
